# Slide Formats

12 predefined format templates for common presentation types.

## 1. Pitch Deck (5-10 slides)

**Structure:**
1. Problem - The pain point you're solving
2. Solution - Your unique approach
3. Traction - Proof it works (metrics, users, revenue)
4. Team - Who's building this
5. Ask - What you need (funding, partners, etc.)

**Use for:** Fundraising, investor meetings, startup competitions

---

## 2. Tutorial (6-12 slides)

**Structure:**
1. Introduction - What they'll learn
2. Prerequisites - What they need to know
3. Steps (3-6 slides) - Core teaching content
4. Practice - Hands-on exercise
5. Recap - Key takeaways
6. Next Steps - Further learning

**Use for:** Training, workshops, educational content

---

## 3. Quarterly Report (8-12 slides)

**Structure:**
1. Executive Summary - High-level wins
2. Key Metrics - Dashboard of numbers
3. Accomplishments - What shipped
4. Challenges - What didn't work
5. Analysis - Why things happened
6. Recommendations - What to do next
7. Roadmap - Next quarter preview
8. Appendix - Supporting data

**Use for:** Business reviews, stakeholder updates

---

## 4. Product Launch (5-8 slides)

**Structure:**
1. Teaser - Hook with the big idea
2. Problem - What users struggle with
3. Features - How your product solves it
4. Demo - Show it working
5. Pricing - How to buy
6. Timeline - When it's available
7. Call to Action - Next steps

**Use for:** Product announcements, marketing launches

---

## 5. Research Presentation (10-15 slides)

**Structure:**
1. Title + Authors
2. Background - Context and related work
3. Research Question - What you're investigating
4. Methodology - How you studied it
5. Results (3-5 slides) - What you found
6. Discussion - What it means
7. Limitations - What to caveat
8. Conclusions - Key findings
9. Future Work - Next research directions

**Use for:** Academic conferences, research reviews

---

## 6. Sales Demo (4-6 slides)

**Structure:**
1. Customer's Pain - Their specific problem
2. Solution Overview - How you solve it
3. Live Demo - Show the product
4. ROI Calculation - Financial impact
5. Social Proof - Customer logos/testimonials
6. Pricing + Next Steps

**Use for:** Sales calls, prospect meetings

---

## 7. Strategy Review (8-10 slides)

**Structure:**
1. Current State - Where we are
2. Market Analysis - External factors
3. SWOT - Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats
4. Strategic Options - Paths we could take
5. Recommendation - Chosen direction
6. Implementation Plan - How to execute
7. Success Metrics - How we'll measure

**Use for:** Leadership meetings, strategic planning

---

## 8. Event Kickoff (3-5 slides)

**Structure:**
1. Welcome - Agenda and goals
2. Context - Why we're here
3. Key Topics - What we'll cover
4. Logistics - Schedule, breaks, WiFi
5. Let's Begin - Transition to first activity

**Use for:** Conference openings, workshop starts

---

## 9. Case Study (6-8 slides)

**Structure:**
1. Client Overview - Who they are
2. Challenge - Their problem
3. Approach - How you helped
4. Solution - What you built/did
5. Results - Measurable outcomes
6. Testimonial - Client quote
7. Learnings - What you'd do again

**Use for:** Portfolio showcases, proof of expertise

---

## 10. Retrospective (5-7 slides)

**Structure:**
1. Sprint/Project Overview - What we did
2. What Went Well - Wins to celebrate
3. What Didn't - Problems we hit
4. Learnings - Insights gained
5. Action Items - Changes for next time

**Use for:** Team retros, post-mortems

---

## 11. Vision Pitch (4-6 slides)

**Structure:**
1. The World Today - Current state
2. The Opportunity - What's possible
3. Our Vision - The future we're building
4. Why Now - Timing and trends
5. Join Us - How to get involved

**Use for:** Mission-driven pitches, recruiting

---

## 12. Lightning Talk (3-5 slides)

**Structure:**
1. Hook - Grab attention in 5 seconds
2. One Big Idea - Core message
3. Why It Matters - Impact
4. Call to Action - What to do

**Use for:** Conference talks, quick updates, standups

---

## Format Selection Guide

| Your goal | Recommended format |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Raise money | Pitch Deck |
| Teach a skill | Tutorial |
| Update executives | Quarterly Report |
| Launch something | Product Launch |
| Share research | Research Presentation |
| Close a deal | Sales Demo |
| Plan strategy | Strategy Review |
| Start an event | Event Kickoff |
| Show your work | Case Study |
| Team reflection | Retrospective |
| Inspire action | Vision Pitch |
| Quick share | Lightning Talk |
